Meet David

A campaign built on neighborhood fights that residents actually felt.

David Suarez is a South Florida native with Cuban and Israeli roots, a University of Florida mechanical engineering graduate, a small business operator, and a father raising his family in Miami Beach.

His public profile grew through local activism, especially the Save SoFi effort and his push to protect residential neighborhoods from overreach, loopholes, and declining quality of life.

This campaign version of the site leans into that identity: direct, energetic, and focused on safer streets, cleaner neighborhoods, infrastructure, accountability, and resident-first leadership.
